UEC International Mini-Conference

About Conference

We are the exchange students from "Japanese University Studies in Science & Technology (JUSST)" program, offered by The University of Electro-Communications (UEC, Tokyo), Japan. This program is specially designed for senior undergraduate (3rd or 4th year students) and graduate students in the field of Informatics, Science and Engineering. We come from many different countries.
